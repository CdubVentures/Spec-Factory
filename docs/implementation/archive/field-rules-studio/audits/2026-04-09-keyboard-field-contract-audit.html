Executive Summary

The contract covers the major keyboard spec landscape well but has significant gaps in known values (especially switch brands and keycap profiles), several unit accuracy issues (gf vs g, kg vs g), and some range bounds that will reject legitimate manufacturer data (battery_life_off, scan_rate). The field structure and grouping are sound. Identity/publish gates are properly configured for core fields.

Top 10 Critical Fixes

#FieldIssueFix
#1 switch_name component.source is 'component_db.sensor' instead of 'component_db.switch', and component.type is 'sensor' instead of 'switch'. This is a data wiring bug that could cause incorrect component matching. Change component.source to 'component_db.switch' and component.type to 'switch'
#2 actuation_force Unit is 'g' but domain standard is 'gf' (gram-force). The same issue affects bottom_out_force. While 'g' is common shorthand, 'gf' is technically correct and avoids confusion with mass. Change contract.unit from 'g' to 'gf' for actuation_force and bottom_out_force. Update UI suffix to match.
#3 weight Unit is 'kg' but virtually all keyboard manufacturers list weight in grams. This creates a unit mismatch between source data and stored values that will require constant conversion. Change unit to 'g' and adjust range to 200-3500
#4 switch_brand Missing Outemu, TTC, Akko, JWK, Tecsee, KTT - some of the most widely used switch manufacturers in the world. Outemu alone is in millions of keyboards globally. Add at minimum: Outemu, TTC, Akko, JWK, Tecsee, KTT, Wooting, Alps, ZealPC
#5 battery_life_off Max of 400 hours will reject many legitimate manufacturer claims. Logitech keyboards routinely claim 2-6 months of battery life, which at 16hr/day can be 1400-2880 hours. Increase max to at least 4000 hours
#6 keycap_profile Missing MT3 (one of the most popular premium profiles from Drop), MDA, ASA, and Flat/Chiclet. These are commonly specified in keyboard reviews and product listings. Add MT3, MDA, ASA, Flat/Chiclet to known values
#7 switch_name Only 15 switch names but hundreds exist in production. Missing Cherry MX Black, Speed Silver, Silent Red, Clear, Green; Gateron Yellow; all Outemu switches; Akko CS line; Wooting Lekker; HyperX switches. Expand to at least 50-80 commonly encountered switches, or ensure component_db coverage is comprehensive
#8 form_factor availability is 'sometimes' but form factor is almost always specified. Also missing '50%' form factor. Change availability to 'expected'. Add '50%' to known values.
#9 mounting_style Missing 'Bottom Mount' and 'Plateless/PCB Mount' which are common mounting styles in both budget and enthusiast keyboards. Add 'Bottom Mount', 'Plateless/PCB Mount' to known values
#10 scan_rate Max 8000Hz is the same as polling_rate but scan rate can be much higher. Wooting scans at up to 40,000Hz. This will reject valid HE keyboard scan rates. Increase max to 50000Hz
